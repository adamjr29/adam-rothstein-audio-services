About Adam Rothstein

Adam Rothstein is a professional audio editor and narrator with years of experience in the industry. Specializing in audiobook narration, podcast editing, and audio editing for videos, Adam brings a meticulous ear and a passion for high-quality sound to every project.

Services

Narrating Audiobooks

Bring your story to life with Adam’s engaging narration. His experience ensures your audiobook captivates listeners from start to finish.

Editing Audiobooks

Enhance your audiobook with professional editing. Adam ensures seamless transitions, clear audio, and a polished final product.

Editing Podcast Episodes

Make your podcast sound professional with Adam’s editing skills. He removes background noise, balances audio levels, and ensures a smooth listening experience.

Editing Audio for Videos

Improve your video’s audio quality with expert editing. Adam can enhance dialogue, remove unwanted noise, and sync audio perfectly with your visuals.
